    How to Use Maps in China as a Foreigner

    Save Chinese addresses for hotels and stations, keep screenshots offline, and use the map app that works best on your phone in China.

    Simple answer

    Save Chinese addresses for hotels and stations, keep screenshots offline, and use the map app that works best on your phone in China.

    Steps

    1. Save hotel and airport names in Chinese and English.
    2. Take screenshots of key addresses before moving between cities.
    3. Use the map tool to open your destination in a supported app.
    4. Show the Chinese address to taxi drivers or hotel staff when needed.
    5. Keep battery and mobile data backups for late-night arrivals.

    Common mistakes

    • Only saving an English hotel name that local drivers do not recognize.
    • Trusting a pin without checking the Chinese address.
    • Forgetting that some international map data can be incomplete.
