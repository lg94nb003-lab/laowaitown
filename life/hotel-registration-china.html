    Hotel Registration in China Explained for Foreign Visitors

    Hotels normally register foreign guests with your passport. If you stay outside a hotel, ask the host how local accommodation registration should be completed.

    Simple answer

    Hotels normally register foreign guests with your passport. If you stay outside a hotel, ask the host how local accommodation registration should be completed.

    Steps

    1. Book accommodation that can accept foreign guests and register passports.
    2. Bring the same passport used for booking and entry.
    3. Ask the front desk to complete temporary accommodation registration.
    4. For private stays, ask the host about local police-station or online registration rules.
    5. Keep your hotel address available for arrival-card and immigration questions.

    Common mistakes

    • Booking a property that cannot complete foreigner registration.
    • Using a different document from the one used to enter China.
    • Assuming private stays have no registration requirement.

    Useful phrase

    I need to register my accommodation.
