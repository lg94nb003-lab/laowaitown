    Best eSIM for China Travel: What to Check Before You Buy

    Choose an eSIM based on coverage, data size, hotspot support, and whether it works with the apps you need during your trip.

    Simple answer

    Choose an eSIM based on coverage, data size, hotspot support, and whether it works with the apps you need during your trip.

    Steps

    1. Check whether your phone supports eSIM and is unlocked.
    2. Buy before departure so you can install over stable Wi-Fi.
    3. Keep the QR code or activation instructions offline.
    4. Test data after landing before leaving the airport.
    5. Keep hotel Wi-Fi as a backup for maps, payments, and ride hailing.

    Common mistakes

    • Buying an eSIM for an unsupported or carrier-locked phone.
    • Forgetting that some eSIMs are data-only and do not include a local phone number.
    • Waiting until arrival without reliable Wi-Fi to activate.
