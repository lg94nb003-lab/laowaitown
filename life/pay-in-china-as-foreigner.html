    How to Pay in China as a Foreigner

    Most foreign visitors should set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival, carry a backup bank card, and keep a small amount of cash for edge cases.

    Simple answer

    Most foreign visitors should set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival, carry a backup bank card, and keep a small amount of cash for edge cases.

    Steps

    1. Install Alipay and WeChat before departure.
    2. Link an international Visa, Mastercard, or supported bank card.
    3. Complete identity verification if the app asks for it.
    4. Keep your passport name consistent across booking, payment, and hotel registration.
    5. Carry a backup card and some cash for small shops or system outages.

    Common mistakes

    • Arriving with only Apple Pay or Google Pay and no China payment app.
    • Using a card blocked for overseas transactions.
    • Ignoring passport-name mismatch during payment-app verification.
