    Guides

    How to Pay in China as a Foreigner

    Most foreign visitors should set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival, carry a backup bank card, and keep a small amount of cash for edge cases.

    Best eSIM for China Travel: What to Check Before You Buy

    Choose an eSIM based on coverage, data size, hotspot support, and whether it works with the apps you need during your trip.

    How to Use Maps in China as a Foreigner

    Save Chinese addresses for hotels and stations, keep screenshots offline, and use the map app that works best on your phone in China.

    China Emergency Numbers for Foreigners

    For immediate danger use 110 police, 119 fire, 120 ambulance, and 122 traffic accident. For immigration and exit-entry questions, use 12367.

    Hotel Registration in China Explained for Foreign Visitors

    Hotels normally register foreign guests with your passport. If you stay outside a hotel, ask the host how local accommodation registration should be completed.
