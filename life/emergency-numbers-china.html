    China Emergency Numbers for Foreigners

    For immediate danger use 110 police, 119 fire, 120 ambulance, and 122 traffic accident. For immigration and exit-entry questions, use 12367.

    Simple answer

    For immediate danger use 110 police, 119 fire, 120 ambulance, and 122 traffic accident. For immigration and exit-entry questions, use 12367.

    Steps

    1. Call 110 for police or immediate personal-safety issues.
    2. Call 119 for fire rescue.
    3. Call 120 for ambulance and medical emergencies.
    4. Call 122 for traffic accidents.
    5. Call 12367 for immigration, visa, stay, and exit-entry service questions.

    Common mistakes

    • Using travel-assistance hotlines for questions meant for China immigration service.
    • Not keeping your hotel address written in Chinese.
    • Waiting to contact your hotel or airline when documents are lost.

    Useful phrase

    I need help. Please call the police.
