    Selected Public Security Articles

    Chinese name: 中华人民共和国治安管理处罚法. These are selected practical articles, not a full law reproduction.

    Article 10: foreigner-specific add-on penalties

    Original excerpt

    对违反治安管理的外国人，可以附加适用限期出境或者驱逐出境。

    English reference: For foreigners who violate public security administration, a time-limit departure order or deportation may be additionally applied.

    Why it matters: A public-security case can become an immigration-status problem, not only a fine or detention issue.

    Article 51: fighting / intentional injury

    Original excerpt

    殴打他人的，或者故意伤害他人身体的，处五日以上十日以下拘留，并处五百元以上一千元以下罚款。

    English reference: Beating another person or intentionally injuring another person's body can lead to five to ten days of detention and a fine from RMB 500 to RMB 1,000.

    Important note: Group fighting, repeated injury, multiple victims, or vulnerable victims can lead to heavier penalties under the same article.

    Article 84: drug use

    Original excerpt

    吸食、注射毒品的，处十日以上十五日以下拘留，可以并处三千元以下罚款；情节较轻的，处五日以下拘留或者一千元以下罚款。

    English reference: Drug use or injection can lead to ten to fifteen days of detention and may include a fine up to RMB 3,000; lighter cases may lead to up to five days of detention or a fine up to RMB 1,000.

    Why it matters: Drug use is not treated as a casual lifestyle issue. It can affect detention, future entry, residence status, and employment.

    Procedure points to know

    Language and checks

    For people who do not understand the local language, the law requires translation support during questioning. Police checks and evidence collection also have procedure rules.

    English reference: If you are involved in a public-security case and cannot understand Chinese, ask for interpretation and keep the written decision documents.
