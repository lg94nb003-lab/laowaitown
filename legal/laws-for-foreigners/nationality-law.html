    Nationality Law

    Chinese name: 中华人民共和国国籍法. The National Immigration Administration publishes an English version of this law for reference.

    Key points

    Nationality status matters for visas

    Nationality determines whether a person is treated as a Chinese citizen, foreign national, or stateless person for entry-exit and consular purposes.

    Naturalization and restoration require approval

    Foreign nationals or stateless persons may apply under statutory grounds, but approval is not automatic.

    Dual nationality

    China's Nationality Law contains rules that make dual-nationality questions sensitive. Individual facts matter, especially for people born abroad with Chinese family ties.
