    Public Security Administration Punishments Law

    Chinese name: 中华人民共和国治安管理处罚法. Revised on June 27, 2025; effective January 1, 2026.

    Why foreigners may care

    This law is relevant background for public-order incidents that are not handled as criminal cases but may still lead to administrative penalties. For foreigners, Article 10 also allows additional time-limit departure or deportation in public-security cases.

    Read these first

    Article 10: foreigner add-on penalties

    Time-limit departure or deportation may be added for foreigners.

    Article 51: fighting / intentional injury

    Detention and fines; heavier situations include group fighting and vulnerable victims.

    Article 84: drug use

    Drug use or injection can lead to detention and fines.

    Procedure: interpretation and documents

    Ask for interpretation if you do not understand Chinese and keep written decisions.

    Official source

    NPC: Public Security Administration Punishments Law

    For any actual detention, penalty notice, or police case, contact a qualified lawyer or the relevant authority.
