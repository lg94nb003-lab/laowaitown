    Civil Code

    Chinese name: 中华人民共和国民法典. This page is a reading guide, not a full translation.

    Why foreigners may care

    The Civil Code can matter for contracts, rentals, consumer disputes, marriage and family, inheritance, personal information/personality rights, tort liability, and everyday civil disputes in China.

    Key areas to read first

    Contracts

    Useful for leases, service agreements, business deals, deposits, cancellation disputes, and consumer transactions.

    Marriage and family

    Useful background for marriage registration, family relationships, property, and divorce questions.

    Tort liability

    Useful for accidents, injuries, property damage, and civil compensation disputes.
