    Can foreigners get married in China?

    Often yes, but documentation, eligibility, notarization, translation, and local registration rules matter.

    Home / Legal / Q&A / Marriage

    Short answer

    Foreigners may be able to register marriage in China if the couple meets legal conditions and local civil-affairs requirements. Exact document rules depend on nationality and registration location.

    Legal basis

    Civil Code, marriage consent principle

    Original excerpt: 结婚应当男女双方完全自愿，禁止任何一方对另一方加以强迫，禁止任何组织或者个人加以干涉。

    English reference: Marriage should be completely voluntary for both parties; coercion by either party and interference by any organization or individual are prohibited.

    Common document themes

    • Passport and valid China stay/residence status.
    • Proof of no impediment or single status from the foreigner's country, often notarized and authenticated.
    • Chinese translation from an accepted translator or agency.
