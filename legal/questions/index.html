    Questions

    Can I work in China on a tourist visa?

    Usually no. Paid work normally requires the correct work authorization and residence status.

    What happens if I overstay in China?

    Overstay can be treated as illegal residence and may lead to fines or other immigration consequences.

    Do I need to register my address?

    Hotels usually register guests; private stays may require registration by you or the host.

    What should I do if I lose my passport?

    Report the loss, contact your embassy or consulate, and ask the local exit-entry office about next steps.

    Can police check my passport?

    Foreigners should keep valid identity and stay documents available for lawful checks.

    Can foreigners get married in China?

    Yes in many cases, but documentation, notarization, and local registration rules matter.
    This section provides general information only. It is not legal advice, and it is not an official interpretation of Chinese law.
