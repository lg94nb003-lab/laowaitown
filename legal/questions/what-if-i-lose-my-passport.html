    What if I lose my passport in China?

    Report the loss, contact your embassy or consulate, and check exit-entry formalities before travel.

    Home / Legal / Q&A / Lost passport

    Short answer

    You usually need a police loss report, replacement travel document from your embassy or consulate, and follow-up with the local exit-entry administration for visa, stay, or departure formalities.

    Practical steps

    1. Report the loss to local police and ask what proof of report is issued locally.
    2. Contact your embassy or consulate for an emergency passport or travel document.
    3. Ask the local exit-entry office whether you need a replacement visa, stay permit, or exit formalities.
    4. Update airlines, hotels, banks, and apps if your passport number changes.
