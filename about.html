    Our Mission

    Help foreign travelers understand China entry policies in the shortest time possible — organized by travel intent rather than policy name.

    We design around the questions travelers actually ask: “Can my passport enter China without a visa?” “Does my route qualify for 240-hour transit?” “How do I pay, navigate, and get around once I arrive?”

    Independence Statement

    LAOWAITOWN is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated on behalf of any Chinese government authority. All information is curated from publicly available official announcements.

    Our goal is to make the information easier to understand, not to replace official sources. Always verify final requirements with the Chinese embassy, consulate, or airline before travel.
