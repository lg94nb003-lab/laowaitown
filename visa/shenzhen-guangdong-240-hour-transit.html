    Shenzhen / Guangdong 240-Hour Visa-Free Transit Guide 2026

    Shenzhen / Guangdong can be a strong 240-hour transit option when you hold an eligible passport and continue to a third country or region within the permitted stay time.

    One-page answer

    You may be able to use 240-hour visa-free transit through Shenzhen / Guangdong if your passport is in the eligible-country list, you enter through a designated port, and you hold confirmed onward transport to a third country or region.

    Eligible ports

    • Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport
    • Guangzhou-Shenzhen-HK Express Rail West Kowloon

    Other Guangdong ports may also be listed for the province-level policy: Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport, Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport, Jieyang Chaoshan International Airport, Nansha Seaport (Passenger), Shekou Seaport (Passenger), Guangzhou Port Pazhou HK-Macao Terminal, Zhongshan Port Passenger Terminal, Hengqin Port, HK-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge Zhuhai Port, Guangzhou-Shenzhen-HK Express Rail West Kowloon.

    Permitted stay area

    Current LAOWAITOWN data lists the permitted area as: Guangdong Province (all open ports).

    For trips involving multiple cities, keep the itinerary simple and confirm whether each city is within the permitted area.

    Documents checklist

    • Valid passport from an eligible 240-hour transit country.
    • Confirmed onward ticket with a fixed date and seat to a third country or region.
    • Hotel address or accommodation plan.
    • Clear itinerary that stays inside the permitted transit area.

    Common refusal risks

    • The route is not a real third-country transit.
    • The onward ticket is missing, open-ended, or not confirmed.
    • The traveler plans to leave the permitted area.
    • The airline is not comfortable boarding the passenger without a visa.

    Example route

    A simple pattern is: Country A -> Shenzhen / Guangdong -> Country B, where Country A and Country B are different countries or regions and the traveler has a confirmed onward ticket.

    FAQ

    Can I use 240-hour visa-free transit in Shenzhen / Guangdong?

    Yes, if your passport nationality, route, port, onward ticket, and stay area meet the official 240-hour transit rules.

    Can I travel outside Guangdong Province (all open ports)?

    Do not assume so. Stay within the permitted area shown by the local 240-hour transit rules and confirm with border inspection if your route crosses administrative boundaries.

    What documents should I prepare?

    Prepare a valid passport, confirmed onward ticket to a third country or region, hotel address, and a clear itinerary.
