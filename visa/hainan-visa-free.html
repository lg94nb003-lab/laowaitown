    Hainan 30-Day Visa-Free Policy 2026: 59 Countries Eligible

    8 min read By LAOWAITOWN Editorial

    Quick Answer

    Citizens of 59 countries can fly directly to Hainan island and stay up to 30 days visa-free, provided activities remain within Hainan province. No advance application is required when arriving on a direct international flight to one of Hainan's three open ports.

    • ✓ 59 nationalities — see full list below
    • ✓ 30 days, refreshed on each new arrival
    • ✓ Activities and movement must stay within Hainan island

    Who is eligible?

    The Hainan visa-free policy is a regional pilot, separate from the unilateral 30-day mainland policy. Eligibility is wider — 59 nationalities versus 50 for mainland — but the geographic scope is limited to Hainan island.

    You qualify if:

    1. You hold an ordinary passport from one of the 59 eligible countries listed below.
    2. You arrive on a direct international flight at one of Hainan's three approved ports (HAK / SYX / BAR).
    3. Your stay will not exceed 30 days and all activities remain within Hainan province.

    Approved entry ports

    You must enter Hainan via a direct international flight at one of three approved ports. Domestic transfers from mainland China do not qualify.

    Sea ports such as Sanya Cruise Terminal serve cruise-only arrivals under a separate cruise visa-free policy.

    Where you can travel

    Movement is permitted across the entire Hainan island — including Sanya, Haikou, Boao, Wenchang, Wuzhishan and the inland mountains. The policy does not cover travel to mainland China.

    • Permitted activities: tourism, business meetings, exhibitions, family / friend visits, medical wellness travel, sports and cultural events.
    • Not permitted: paid employment, study leading to a degree, news reporting, or onward travel to mainland China without an additional visa.

    If you wish to combine Hainan with a mainland city, apply for a regular L (tourism) or M (business) visa before departure — visa-free entry to Hainan cannot be converted into a mainland visa on arrival.

    Group tour or independent traveller?

    Hainan's visa-free policy applies to both group and individual travellers. Travellers in approved tour groups must remain with the group itinerary; independent travellers can move freely within Hainan island.

    • Independent traveller: book your own flights, hotels and itinerary. Show passport and onward ticket on arrival.
    • Tour group: the registered travel agency files an advance notification with Hainan immigration; you simply follow the itinerary.

    Common mistakes to avoid

    • Routing through Beijing or Shanghai. A connecting flight via mainland China does not qualify. You must arrive on a direct international flight to HAK, SYX or BAR.
    • Trying to visit mainland China after Hainan. Hainan visa-free entry cannot be extended to the mainland. For a multi-city China trip, apply for a regular tourism (L) visa before departure.
    • Confusing Hainan visa-free with the 30-day unilateral policy. If your nationality is on the unilateral list (e.g. France, Germany, Australia), you can use either — but the unilateral version is more flexible (full mainland access).
    • Overstaying the 30 days. Apply for an extension at the Hainan provincial Exit-Entry Administration before the deadline, or face fines from ¥500 per day up to ¥10,000 and a future entry record.
    • Skipping accommodation registration. Hotels handle this automatically; if you stay at a private residence or short-term rental, register at the local police station within 24 hours.

    Frequently asked questions

    Can I fly from Hainan to mainland China while still visa-free?

    No. Hainan visa-free entry is geographically restricted to Hainan island. To visit mainland China, you must hold a separate mainland visa or qualify for the unilateral 30-day or 240-hour transit policies on your initial entry.

    How do I extend my stay beyond 30 days?

    Apply at the Hainan provincial Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration before your deadline expires. Bring passport, hotel address and a written reason. Extensions are at the officer's discretion and typically grant up to 30 additional days.

    Can I enter Hainan on a connecting flight from another Chinese city?

    No. The Hainan visa-free policy requires a direct international flight to HAK, SYX or BAR. Domestic legs through Beijing, Shanghai or Guangzhou disqualify you. Cruise arrivals at Sanya use the separate cruise visa-free policy.

    Does the Belt & Road wellness travel programme give extra time?

    Approved international medical tourism patients at the Boao Lecheng Pilot Zone may receive longer stays under separate medical visa procedures, but standard Hainan visa-free entry is capped at 30 days.

    Can I work remotely from Hainan during 30 days visa-free?

    Working remotely for a foreign employer is generally tolerated, but any paid work for a Chinese entity requires a work (Z) visa. Hainan immigration may question travellers who repeatedly use back-to-back 30-day entries to live and work on the island.

    What if I want to combine Hainan with Hong Kong?

    You can fly Hainan → Hong Kong, since Hong Kong is treated as a separate region. Hong Kong has its own visa-free arrangements for most western passports (up to 90 days). Returning to mainland China from Hong Kong requires a separate visa or visa-free eligibility.
