China Visa-Free Entry for US Citizens 2026

Quick answer before you book your China trip: the US is not in the 30-day visa-free list, but 240-hour transit may work for the right route.

Calculate stay dates Open phrasebook

Quick answer

Verified
30-day entryNo
240h transitYes

HainanYes

What this means

US passport holders should not rely on the normal 30-day visa-free route in this example. For a short China stop, compare 240-hour transit, 24-hour transit, Hainan entry, or a regular visa.

Conditions

  • Eligible passport and route.
  • Confirmed onward ticket.
  • Stay inside allowed area.

Documents

  • Passport.
  • Hotel address.
  • Return or onward booking.

Common risks

  • Wrong transit route.
  • Unclear itinerary.
  • Counting stay dates incorrectly.

Step-by-step

Check whether your passport and purpose match the route you want to use.

Confirm your arrival city, allowed stay area, and onward destination.

Use the stay calculator before booking flights and hotels.

Prepare a short explanation and keep documents ready at entry.
